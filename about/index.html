About Us

The Executive Director has a passion for entrepreneurship and devotion to the construction industry, which fuels his contributions to society through business, community volunteerism and local and regional employment. He is a philanthropist contributing to community development projects and commercial enterprise.
Mr Jagdip has three decades of expertise in the construction and glazing sector, where he managed many projects for reputable companies throughout Trinidad and Tobago and the Caribbean. In 2005 he embarked on the entrepreneurial journey in the construction sector and began independent project managing and supplying of goods under the Glass and Cladding Expert Limited title.
The company quickly grew from a small enterprise to an established import, specialization and distribution operation. His clientele ranges from small business firms and residential clients to large commercial and governmental developments.
The Executive Director has gained a wealth of knowledge on the available technologies in the field of construction and have acquired hands on training and experience in the cladding and glazing sector in an international environment. Over the years he has acquired certificates from institutions in China and Trinidad, such as: AAT Level 1 – Association of Accounting technicians – 1997 ; Glazing Training at Glass Build America’s Show – 2006 -2012 ; Certificate in glass training in Quintoa, China – 2008 ; Certificate in Cladding from Ibond Facility in Shanghai – 2008 ; Occupational Health and Safety Certificate – 2012 ; Aluminum training certificate in Wechang – 2013 ; Certificate in Aluminum Specialization in Ghaunghoz, China – 2014 ; Certificate in Cladding Training Facilities in Alubang, China – 2014 ; Professional Certificate in Managing Construction Projects, SBCS, Trinidad – 2016 ; and Masters of Business Administration (MBA) in Leadership and Management in 2018.
